Our Purpose

Namma Kudla was created to help people explore Mangalore in a simple and friendly way. We focus on beaches, temples, food spots, and real travel experiences.

What You'll Find Here

  • Easy travel guides
  • Simple blogs

Why Kudla?

Kudla (Mangalore) is known for its calm beaches, unique cuisine, and rich cultural vibe. We wanted to make a small website that showcases this beauty without being too flashy.
