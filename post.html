Exploring Panambur Beach

Published on: Jan 12, 2025

Panambur Beach is one of Mangalore's most visited beaches, known for its golden sand, clear waters, and breathtaking sunsets. It's a perfect spot for families and travelers looking to enjoy water sports, camel rides, and delicious beachside snacks.

The beach also hosts several events like the annual International Kite Festival, which attracts people from around the world. Whether you're planning a calm evening walk or an adventurous weekend, Panambur Beach has something for everyone.

Highlights

  • Water sports and activities
  • Clean and well-maintained surroundings
  • Perfect for sunset photography
  • Nearby food stalls with local snacks
