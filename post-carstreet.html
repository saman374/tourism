Walking Through Carstreet

Published on: Jan 28, 2025

Carstreet in Mangalore is one of the most vibrant and culturally rich areas of Kudla. Known for its ancient temples, colorful markets, and traditional shops, a walk through Carstreet feels like stepping into the heart of Mangalorean heritage.

The famous Sri Venkataramana Temple stands at the center, attracting devotees from all over Karnataka. During festivals like Car Festival (Teru), the entire street lights up with music, traditions, and thousands of people coming together in celebration.

What You'll See in Carstreet

  • Traditional gold and jewelry shops
  • Old-style flower stalls with fresh jasmine garlands
  • Cultural spots and ancient architecture
  • Local sweet shops serving classics like laddoos and payasam

Whether you're a history lover, a foodie, or a casual traveler, Carstreet offers a magical blend of culture, devotion, and authentic Kudla charm. A simple evening walk here stays with you forever.
